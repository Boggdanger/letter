Eiffel Tower Guided Climb
Please meet us outside the Eiffel Tower and do not pass through any security checks. You will find us outside the South Security Entrance (Entrance #1) of the Eiffel Tower on Allée des Refuzniks. (The Allée des Refuzniks is a dirt footpath just outside of the Eiffel Tower near the trees and the benches.)
For easier navigation, you can paste these coordinates into Google Maps: 48.857358,2.293079 .
The check-in starts 30 minutes before the start time. Please arrive at least 15 minutes before your tour time because the tour usually starts on time. If you booked through your Paris pass, please have your Paris passes readily available so that you can get checked in by our staff and get your passes scanned. For more details, visit our welcome page .
